Vision

It doesn’t interest me what you do for a living. I want to know what you ache for and if you dare to dream of meeting your heart’s longing.

{% include "partials/processes_nav.html" %}

We want to create a space where we can explore computer science and software engineering.

Space is both spatial and temporal—and we’re missing both kinds. Very few empty buildings to house us, very little time (of no livelihood-making) to spend in exploring knowledge and talking with each other.

Chaitin School wants to be this space, for people to explore ideas, to try experiments and fail, to make jokes, to participate, and to create new connections and networks.
