What we do

We set up events to get better at programming. Currently, we organise a reading group and an open source hack night every ~2 weeks.

Find all events in our events page.

We plan to do more workshops in the future with coding-oriented content. We are not interested in lecture-style presentations or invited talks. Our aim is to explore a topic all together. Having expertise is something we welcome but it’s not a requirement.
