Community Office Hours

During our Hack Night events we hold community office hours where anyone can drop by and discuss their technical problem.

Contrary to traditional university office hours, there are no fixed roles of professors and students. Everyone* is welcome to attend and either ask or provide a possible answer.

If you’re new, check out our Code of Conduct.

✱ Everyone as in literally everyone. People new to programming, people old to programming, old people in general, young people in general, people who think “everyone” in this paragraph does not include them, and people who think the previous clause does not include them.

If you have any accessibility needs, let us know.

If you are not sure you can attend Community Office Hours, send us an email.

For any questions in general, send us an email.
